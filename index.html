My Work

UX Concept – Dynamic delta attribute filters at idealo.de

This UX concept done within the Human Centered Design method has the purpose to help users to find the right product variant with the right delta attributes. The display of unclear static product variants is replaced by easily understandable delta attribute filters. Axure was used to design the prototype.

UX Concept – Help mobile users to find similar products on the product page

This UX concept done within the Human Centered Design method has the purpose to help mobile users browsing at idealo by showing alternative products on the product page, resulting in a substantial decrease of the bounce rate as well as an increase in user engagement. Sketch was used to design the prototype.
